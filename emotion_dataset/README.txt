*******************************************************************************************************************
        		Swahili Emotion Dataset
*******************************************************************************************************************
The dataset consists of emotion annotated comments from social media platforms and translated English dataset. 
The data is multi-label with six Ekmanâ€™s emotions:  happy, surprise, sadness, fear, anger, and disgust or neutral.


* The dataset consist of 12,976 instances obtained from two source type:
       1) Social media platforms (JamiiForum, YouTube, and Twitter) and
       2) English Emotion dataset (Dailydialog, Emotion cause, and ISEAR)


 * The dataset consist of three CSV files: emo_train, emo_valid, and emo_test, each with data in ratio of 75%, 10%, and 15%, respectively.
	Size of training dataset: 9,732
	Size of validation dataset: 1,297
	Size of test dataset: 1,947
 
 * Each file contains columns of text and label id(s) (the ids are indexed based on the order of emotions below) 
  	0 - neutral
	1 - joy (furaha)
	2 - anger (hasira)
	3 - sadness (huzuni)
	4 - disgust (machukizo)
	5 - suprise (mshangao)
	6 - fear (woga)


 *** More details could be found in the paper "SwahBERT: Language Model of Swahili".
